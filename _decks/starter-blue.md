## Overview

Starter Blue is an excellent introductory deck for players new to Sorcery TCG. This deck emphasizes water's core strengths: card advantage through draw spells, creature control, and efficient creatures that protect your life total while advancing your game plan.

## Deck Strategy

This deck uses a control-focused approach with water creatures and removal spells:

- **Early Game**: Establish board presence with efficient creatures like {{ "Coy Nixie" | card_link }} and {{ "Order of the Sacred Oak" | card_link }}
- **Mid Game**: Draw cards with {{ "Mariner's Curse" | card_link }} and control your opponent's threats using removal spells
- **Late Game**: Apply pressure with larger creatures or control the board with {{ "Waveshaper" | card_link }} effects

### Creatures (Minions)

### Sites & Lands
- Multiple water sites for mana foundation

## Gameplay Tips

1. **Manage Your Spellbook**: Track which spells you've drawn and plan your plays accordingly
2. **Tempo Advantage**: Use {{ "Mariner's Curse" | card_link }} early to gain card advantage
3. **Efficient Trading**: Use {{ "Tufted Turtles" | card_link }} and {{ "Shellycoat" | card_link }} to block and control the board
4. **Waveshaper Control**: Use your {{ "Waveshaper" | card_link }} avatar to tempo out larger threats

## Variations

As you become more comfortable with the deck, consider these upgrades:

- Add more card draw spells like Undertow
- Include bigger finishers like creatures with higher power
- Experiment with different removal options based on the meta
- Optimize your mana base with dual-site support for consistency
